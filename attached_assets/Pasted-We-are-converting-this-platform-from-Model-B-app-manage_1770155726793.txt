We are converting this platform from Model B (app-managed recurring) to Model A (Helcim-managed automatic billing).

🚨 PRIMARY GOAL

The app must NOT run billing jobs or initiate recurring charges.
Helcim must handle all recurring billing automatically.

Our app should:

Configure subscriptions

Store Helcim references (IDs)

Listen to webhooks

Update invoices/ledger/access

2️⃣ Required Outcomes (Non-Negotiable)
A) Monthly Automatic Billing (Client Chooses Date)

Clients (clubs and/or parents) must be able to choose a billing day (1–28)

Helcim must automatically charge every month on that date

No cron jobs, no manual triggers

B) One-Time Charges

Support one-time event billing in either of these ways (choose the cleanest):

Helcim one-time “purchase” payment

OR Helcim recurring add-ons (one-time add-on to subscription)

C) Security / Compliance

❌ Never store raw card or bank numbers

✅ Storing Helcim customerId, subscriptionId, or planId is OK

Tokens are Helcim vault tokens only

Monthly
