========================
PRICING RULES (SOURCE OF TRUTH)
========================
Define:
- baseAmount = the amount the club is charging for the item (contract installment or event fee)
- monthsCount = number of months being paid in this checkout (1 for monthly, 3 for quarterly, 9 for season upfront, etc.)
- paymentRail = "card_credit" | "card_debit" | "ach"
- paymentKind = "recurring_contract" | "one_time_event"

A) Recurring contract payments (installments / season payments):
- Card CREDIT: techFee = roundToCents(baseAmount * 0.03) + (3.00 * monthsCount)
- Card DEBIT:  techFee = (3.00 * monthsCount)   // NO PERCENT
- ACH:         techFee = roundToCents(baseAmount * 0.015) + (3.00 * monthsCount)

B) One-time payments (tournament, clinic, event, drop-in, etc.):
- Card CREDIT: techFee = roundToCents(baseAmount * 0.03) + 1.00
- Card DEBIT:  techFee = 1.00    // NO PERCENT
- ACH:         techFee = roundToCents(baseAmount * 0.015) + 1.00

Total Charged to payer:
totalAmount = baseAmount + techFee

Display requirements:
- Show “Technology and Service Fees” line item.
- Show two totals before payment selection:
  - “Pay by Card: $X (includes Technology and Service Fees)”
  - “Pay by ACH: $Y (ACH Discount Applied)”
- If the payer uses DEBIT, remove the percent and show:
  - “Debit Card: Technology and Service Fees = $flat only”
