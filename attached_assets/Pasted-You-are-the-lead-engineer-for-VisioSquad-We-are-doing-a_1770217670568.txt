PRICING RULES (SINGLE SOURCE OF TRUTH)
========================
Define:
- baseAmount = the amount the club is charging for the item
- monthsCount = number of months covered by this checkout (1, 3, 9, etc.)
- paymentKind = 'recurring_contract' | 'one_time_event'
- paymentRail = 'card_credit' | 'card_debit' | 'ach'

Recurring contract payments:
- Card CREDIT: techFee = roundToCents(baseAmount * 0.03) + (3.00 * monthsCount)
- Card DEBIT:  techFee = (3.00 * monthsCount)           // NO PERCENT
- ACH:         techFee = roundToCents(baseAmount * 0.015) + (3.00 * monthsCount)

One-time payments (tournament/clinic/event/drop-in):
- Card CREDIT: techFee = roundToCents(baseAmount * 0.03) + 1.00
- Card DEBIT:  techFee = 1.00                           // NO PERCENT
- ACH:         techFee = roundToCents(baseAmount * 0.015) + 1.00

Total charged to payer:
totalAmount = baseAmount + techFee

Fee config must be adjustable (do NOT hardcode in multiple places):
- CARD_PERCENT = 0.03
- ACH_PERCENT = 0.015
- RECURRING_FLAT_PER_MONTH = 3.00
- ONE_TIME_FLAT = 1.00
